Questions

Frequently asked questions.

The things business owners most often want to know before getting in touch.

How much does a website cost?

Every project is quoted according to its size, content and functionality. After a short consultation you'll receive a clear fixed quote outlining exactly what is included.

How long does it take?

Most small business websites are completed within approximately three to six weeks depending on project size and content readiness.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes — redesigns are a core part of what we do. We start with a free Forge Score™ health check to pinpoint what's working and what's costing you enquiries, then rebuild around your customers so the new site is clearer, faster and better at generating leads.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes. Our Care service includes fast, secure hosting along with ongoing maintenance and support, so your site stays online, up to date and performing without you having to think about it.

What happens after I enquire?

We respond within one business day to arrange a short, no-pressure conversation about your goals. From there we run your free Forge Score™ health check and share a prioritised action plan — so even if we never work together, you leave with something useful.
